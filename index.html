Justice for Juniors

Justice for Juniors is a CMS application created during Ruby For Good conference. Justice for Juniors is dedicated to addressing juvenile recidivism in the District of Columbia by offering mentoring and tutorial visitation services to juveniles returning to the community in subjects such as ethics, education, and faith. Dedicated to make the world gooder.

  • Date: May 2017
  • Collaborators: Rafael Garcial, Emily Bosakowski, Kevin Konikowski, Ali Topaloglu, Jennifer Konikowski, Anissa Jones, Malissa Cruz Romero
